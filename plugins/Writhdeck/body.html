About Writhdeck

Writhdeck is a distraction-free writing environment for long-form text — novels, essays, notes, scripts.

All your documents live in your browser (IndexedDB) — nothing is sent to any server. You can also edit files directly on disk.

It features a custom colour-syntax overlay, typewriter focus mode, a session timer, daily word goals, and a fully keyboard-driven interface. Compatible with the Writhdeck desktop app via shared .ini settings.

Build: 2026-06-19 — Open source — github.com/luginf/writhdeck-web
